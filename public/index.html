Period Pain Comfort Lounge 🌸

Instant pain relief methods, premium soothing items (roll-ons, heat patches, TENS massagers, and organic pads) and how to use them to get relief.

Quick Relief

Period Cramp Roll-ons

Suggested: Pee Safe Cramp Relief Roll-on / Nua Cramp Comfort

Formulated with natural cooling and soothing extracts like Menthol, Eucalyptus oil, and Wintergreen oil. It absorbs quickly into the skin to relieve muscle spasms.

How to use to get relief:
  1. Roll gently over your lower abdomen, lower back, or thighs where you feel cramping.
  2. Let it absorb. It creates a comforting warm-cool sensation in 5-10 minutes.
  3. Reapply every 3-4 hours as needed.
8-Hour Comfort

Thermal Cramp Relief Patches & Tapes

Suggested: Pee Safe Feminine Cramp Relief Patch / Nua Heat Patch

Air-activated patches that emit constant, gentle heat (around 40°C) to relax uterine muscles and improve blood flow, mimicking a hot water bottle on-the-go.

How to use to get relief:
  1. Open the packaging to activate the thermal cells.
  2. Peel off the backing paper and stick the patch onto the outside of your underwear (never apply directly to bare skin to prevent burns).
  3. Wear it for up to 8 hours for continuous, portable soothing warmth.
Advanced Tech

Period Pain TENS Massager Devices

Suggested: Welme Period Pain Relief Device / Pulse Stimulators

A wearable, drug-free device that sends safe, micro-current electrical pulses to stimulate nerves, blocking pain signals to the brain and triggering endorphin release.

How to use to get relief:
  1. Place the gel pads on your lower abdomen or lower back where the pain is concentrated.
  2. Connect the device and turn it on, adjusting the intensity to a comfortable pulse level.
  3. Use it for 20-30 minute sessions. You can wear it under your clothes while working or walking.
Pure Protection

Organic & Natural Sanitary Pads

Suggested: Nua Organic Cotton Pads / Carmesi Bamboo Pads

Made from 100% certified organic cotton or biodegradable bamboo fibers. Free from chlorine bleach, dioxins, synthetic fragrances, and plastics, preventing skin rashes.

How to use to get relief:
  1. Choose the correct absorbency size (Light, Heavy, XL) corresponding to your flow.
  2. Change pads every 4-6 hours, even on lighter flow days, to maintain hygiene.
  3. Store in a clean, dry place to prevent moisture from compromising the natural fibers.

Myth vs Fact 💡

Myth

PCOD means you are infertile and cannot have children.

Fact

FALSE. PCOD causes irregular ovulation, meaning it may take longer to conceive naturally, but with lifestyle changes and simple support, most women can become pregnant.

Myth

Period pain is completely normal; you just have to deal with it.

Fact

Mild cramping is normal. However, severe, debilitating pain that forces you to miss school or work is NOT normal and could indicate conditions like endometriosis. Talk to a gynecologist.
